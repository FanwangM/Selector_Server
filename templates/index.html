About Procrustes Analysis

Procrustes analysis is a form of statistical shape analysis that is used to analyze the distribution of a set of shapes. The analysis involves superimposing the shapes by optimally translating, rotating, and uniformly scaling them.

Available Methods

  • Orthogonal Procrustes: Finds an orthogonal transformation matrix that most closely maps one matrix to another.
  • Rotational Procrustes: Similar to orthogonal Procrustes but restricted to rotations only.
  • Permutation Procrustes: Finds the best permutation of rows/columns to align two matrices.
